# Owner Operating System - Automacao CAD

This document defines the execution model for the owner/CEO.
The goal is simple: grow revenue with predictable delivery quality.

## 1) North Star

Primary target: Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

Supporting targets:

- New MRR growth
- Activation rate (new account reaches first value in 7 days)
- Weekly active accounts
- Gross churn
- Platform uptime and incident rate

## 2) Weekly Cadence

Every week has four fixed rituals:

1. Monday - Executive Scorecard (60 min)

- Review business and delivery numbers.
- Decide top 3 priorities for the week.
- Assign one owner per priority.

2. Tuesday - Product and GTM sync (45 min)

- Review funnel conversion and product friction.
- Align product work with revenue opportunities.

3. Wednesday - Reliability and Security review (45 min)

- Review incidents, SLA/SLO, regressions, security risks.
- Define hardening tasks for the next release window.

4. Friday - Commit review and close (45 min)

- Check what moved metrics this week.
- Close wins, blockers, and carry-over actions.

## 3) Decision Framework

Use this order for prioritization:

1. Revenue impact
2. Retention impact
3. Reliability and security risk reduction
4. Delivery speed

If an initiative does not improve at least one top metric in 30 days, pause it.

## 4) 90-Day Execution Tracks

Track A: Revenue Engine

- Build predictable pipeline by channel.
- Improve conversion from pricing to checkout.
- Improve onboarding to first value in 7 days.

Track B: Product Experience

- Standardize enterprise UX for core operational journeys.
- Reduce high-friction steps in CAD/CAM workflow.
- Improve role-based admin and audit usability.

Track C: Platform Reliability

- Keep error budgets visible and enforce release quality gates.
- Reduce MTTR and incident recurrence.
- Stabilize background processing and integrations.

Track D: Governance

- Maintain scorecard discipline.
- Keep ownership clear per priority.
- Keep quarterly OKRs realistic and measurable.

## 5) Weekly Scorecard Fields

Required scorecard fields:

- New qualified leads
- New trials started
- Activation in 7 days (%)
- Trial to paid conversion (%)
- New MRR
- Expansion MRR
- Churn MRR
- NRR (%)
- Weekly active accounts
- Uptime (%)
- P1/P2 incidents
- Mean time to resolve (MTTR)
- Deployment success rate (%)

## 6) First 4 Weeks (Immediate Execution)

Week 1

- Baseline all scorecard metrics.
- Define owners by business track.
- Start weekly executive ritual.

Week 2

- Fix top 3 conversion blockers in pricing/checkout/onboarding.
- Publish reliability baseline and incident policy.

Week 3

- Launch one expansion offer for current paid accounts.
- Close top 5 quality/regression risks affecting core flows.

Week 4

- Compare baseline vs current metrics.
- Keep, kill, or scale initiatives based on data.

## 7) Owner Rules

- No roadmap item without metric hypothesis.
- No release without rollback and monitoring.
- No strategic drift from weekly scorecard truth.
- Keep strategy in quarterly cycles, execution in weekly cycles.
